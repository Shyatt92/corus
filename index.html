Our Location

Corus Hotel Hyde Park offers accommodation close to everything there is to see or experience in London. Located across from Hyde Park within walking distance of Lancaster Gate, the hotel is just minutes away from Paddington Station and the Heathrow Express. From the hotel, it’s just moments to fabulous shopping on Oxford Street, Bond Street and Knightsbridge, the museums and art galleries in South Kensington and world-famous London attractions including Buckingham Palace, Leicester Square and the City of Westminster.

Located in an elegant 200-year-old listed Georgian mansion in the fashionable Hyde Park neighbourhood of London. Corus Hotel Hyde Park offers discreet yet warm, friendly and genuine service combined with four-star comforts and amenities to make you feel completely at home.

Designed for modern business and leisure travellers, our 4 star hotel in central London features bustling, energetic spaces for working, socialising, dining and relaxing. Remarkably affordable residential-style rooms and suites feature a classic décor and thoughtful little luxuries like complimentary Wi-Fi, flat-screen televisions with satellite channels and plush, pillow top beds.

With three bright, contemporary meeting spaces accommodating up to 20 delegates, we can host events designed to impress and inspire. Enjoy authentic British and Malaysian fare at The Brasserie at Hyde Park,  or gather for cocktails and European-style snacks in the inviting Bar.
